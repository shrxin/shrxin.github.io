About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong University. I am advised by Prof. Jun Liu and affiliated with Shaanxi Provincial Key Laboratory of Big Data Knowledge Engineering. Besides my supervisor, I also work closely with Dr. Lingling Zhang. Before I becoming a Ph.D. student, I also received my bachelor and master degrees from Northwestern Polytechnical University. Here is my CV.

Research

My research interests lie within the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning. More specifically, I am currently interested in few/zero-shot learning, pre-trained language models and information extraction.
